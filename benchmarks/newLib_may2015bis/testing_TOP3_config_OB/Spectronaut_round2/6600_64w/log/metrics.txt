 Metrics description: 
	identification:
		identification rate,
		the number of identified proteins for benchmark species
	replication:
		replication variance,
		the median CV for the background species
	accuracy:
		accuracy of relative quantification,
		the area under quantification curve for background species
	precision deviation:
		deviation of precision of quantification,
		the root mean square of ( medianLR - expectation ) for regulated species
	separation:
		species separation ability,
		the area under ROC curve between a species pair
	separation by range:
		species separation ability for predefined log2-intensity ranges,
		the area under ROC curve between a species pair
